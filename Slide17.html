Case Study 02 • Financial Services

Building One Culture from Two Legacies

Post-Merger Entity

Following a major acquisition, the merged entity faced "organ rejection." Legacy teams operated in silos, hoarding information and resisting integration efforts, threatening key talent retention.

The Challenge
  • • Severe cultural clash paralyzing decision-making speed.
  • • High flight risk of critical leadership talent (top 100).
  • • Integration timeline slipping (projected 18 months).
The Intervention
  • • Cross-functional coaching to break down legacy silos.
  • • "One Enterprise" narrative co-created by top 50 leaders.
  • • Conflict transformation protocols for merger friction.
