Case Study 04 • Financial Services & Banking

Building Digital Capability In a Legacy Institution

Private Sector Bank (Top 15, ₹180k Cr Assets)

Digital banking initiative stalled for 2 years due to a deep tech-business divide and frontline resistance to channel shift.

The Challenge
  • • Execution Silos: IT delivering features, Business not adopting.
  • • Channel Conflict: Branch network resisting migration.
  • • Fragmented executive alignment (6 business heads).
The Intervention
  • • Executive Coaching: Creating a unified steering coalition.
  • • Business-IT "Bridge Workshops" to co-create roadmaps.
  • • Branch Manager Capability Program (Advisory vs Transactional).

Business Insight

Adoption drives economics, not technology deployment. The people layer unlocks the P&L.
