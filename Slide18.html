Case Study 03 • Manufacturing & Industrial

Scaling Operations Without Losing Agility

Industrial Conglomerate (₹3,500 Cr)

Rapid expansion (12 to 23 plants) strained the operating model, leading to rising safety incidents and quality drift.

The Challenge
  • • Growth Pains: Doubling footprint caused diluted oversight.
  • • Quality Dip: First-pass yield dropped below 80%.
  • • Siloed plant leadership; no sharing of best practices.
The Intervention
  • • Cross-Plant Coaching: Creating a network of operational leaders.
  • • Standard Operating Rhythm design driven by plant managers.
  • • Capability transfer model (Plant-to-Plant).

Business Insight

We treated leadership capability as competitive infrastructure—just as critical as the machinery.
