Case Study 01 • Technology Services

From Agile Theater to Intrinsic Agility

Large Tech Services Firm

The client had invested heavily in agile tools (Jira, DevOps) but saw no improvement in speed. The transformation was "stuck" at the process layer without penetrating behavior.

The Challenge
  • • Failed agile rollout with a pre-intervention 18-month delay in value realization.
  • • "Agile in name only" — teams used new terms for old behaviors.
  • • Leadership viewed transformation as an "IT project."
The Intervention
  • • Systemic Team Coaching for 12 pilot squads to build psychological safety.
  • • Shifted focus from "Tooling" to "Teaming" protocols.
  • • Executive alignment workshops to redefine sponsorship.

Business Insight

Alignment + Team Coaching converted "tooling investment" into actual "delivery throughput."
Transformation speed is limited by human adoption, not software capability.
