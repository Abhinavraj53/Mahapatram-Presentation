Case Study 05 • Life Sciences & Pharma

From Hierarchical to Networked Innovation

Global Pharma India R&D Center

Despite a large talent pool (2,400 scientists), the innovation pipeline was stagnating due to functional silos (Chemistry vs. Biology vs. Clinical) and decision bottlenecks.

The Challenge
  • • Pipeline Stagnation: 6-year development cycles (vs 4-year benchmark).
  • • Talent Crisis: 23% attrition in critical PI roles.
  • • Risk-averse culture stifling hypothesis generation.
The Intervention
  • • Leadership Mindset Shift: Moving from "Command & Control" to "Enable & Empower."
  • • Cross-functional coaching for 12 core drug development teams.
  • • Stage-gate process simplification (Decision speed).

Business Insight

Leadership behavior change directly unlocked innovation velocity.
Psychological safety is the R&D engine.
