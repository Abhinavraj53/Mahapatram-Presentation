Research Readout

Short pilots validated tools but rarely changed cross-team habits or governance cadence.

ROI accelerated after leaders embedded rituals and middle layers began self-correcting.

Duration worked only when learning loops were translated into internal ownership.

Analyst Conclusion

Time alone does not create ROI. Structured duration with capability transfer creates compounding enterprise return.
